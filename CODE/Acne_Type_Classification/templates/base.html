1. Clinical Data Privacy Statement

At Acne Ai, we implement state-of-the-art secure transmission and encryption protocols to protect dermatological imagery. Uploaded clinical images are processed in-memory for model inference and are deleted instantly unless saved within your secure personal dashboard history.

2. HIPAA Alignment & Data Security

All personal details, history datasets, and predicted diagnostics are secured under AES-256 standards. Under no circumstances is clinical diagnostic data sold or shared with third-party networks.

1. Educational Assistance License

The diagnostic predictions, spot detection, and severity scores rendered by Acne Ai are supported by a hybrid deep learning model (VGG16 + ResNet50 + YOLOv5). These results are educational/assisting assets intended to empower patients and must not be treated as absolute medical prescriptions.

2. Limitation of Clinical Liability

Acne Ai shall not be held liable for any self-treatment decisions made by the user. Users are explicitly advised to consult certified dermatologists for definitive treatment regimes.

1. Diagnostic Tracking Intervals

Under the Terms of Calendar, diagnostic records are timestamped and formatted as a timeline directory. This allows dermatological tracking over custom dates, weekly progress parameters, and monthly recovery metrics.

2. Automatic Log Maintenance

Registered diagnostic logs remain indexed within the user's secure calendar pipeline indefinitely, or until manually deleted via the history management interface.
